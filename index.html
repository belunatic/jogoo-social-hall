About Us

At Jogoo Social Hall, we specialize in transforming everyday gatherings into unforgettable celebrations. Every event—whether a wedding, birthday, or corporate function—is treated as a once-in-a-lifetime experience.Our team is dedicated to perfecting every detail, ensuring your occasion is seamless, stylish, and truly extraordinary.

Our spacious venue comfortably accommodates up to 350 guests, with additional room for dancing and an outdoor lawn ideal for up to 50 more. Whether you're planning a daytime ceremony or an elegant evening reception, we’ve got you covered—with reliable backup power and ambient lighting that brings nighttime events to life.

From weddings, engagement parties, and graduations to charity fundraisers, baby showers, and corporate events, Jogoo Social Hall is your go-to destination for hosting memorable moments.

If you're looking for a versatile, welcoming, and professionally managed event space in Mbezi Beach, Dar-es-Salaam, we’re ready to make your vision a reality.

Amenities

Venue Capacity

Jogoo Social Hall offers spacious accommodations for up to 350 guests and vendors, with additional room designed specifically for dancing and entertainment. Our beautifully maintained lawn area can host up to 50 guests and is available for tented setups—please note, only non-staked tents are permitted to preserve the integrity of the grounds.

Venue Amenities

Jogoo Social Hall is equipped with a commercial refrigerator and freezers located inside the bar area, ensuring reliable cold storage for catered events and refreshments. The venue includes three restroom stalls for women and six for men, providing ample facilities for guests. Convenient parking spaces are available directly outside the hall, offering easy access and added comfort for all attendees.

Additional Amenities

Jogoo Social Hall offers a range of thoughtful amenities to enhance your event experience, including onsite parking and professional security guards for peace of mind. Tent rentals and event planning services are available upon request to support your unique vision. Guests can enjoy a full-service bar area with comfortable seating, all within a clean and smoke-free environment designed for comfort and celebration.
